About Me...

I design and develop web sites!

My name is Brandan Majeske; I live in Boise, Idaho. I love to work on web sites and web apps! It's the creative challenges that come along with each new project that keep me coming back. There's nothing like facing a challenge, solving a problem or ten along the way, and being able to say, "See that marvelous thing there? I did that!" In short, I love to design and hand-code beautiful little corners of 'the web'!

My web-education started about five years ago with a desire to create my own blog. I didn't know much about web servers or mark up languages, but I was determined to learn! I started to learn HTML, CSS and eventually some PHP. Today I am happy to say I am quite comfortable with what makes the web work. I recently graduated with honors from Full Sail University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Web Design and Development.

My design philosophy is user-centric; I know who I need to have in mind when I sit down to create a site or app. If it isn't usable, it isn't much good - no matter how pretty it is! I've spent a fair amount of time studying what makes a good User Experience. A great experience is the aim of all my design intentions.
